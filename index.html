About Me

I'm a Master's student in Data Science at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth , working as a Graduate Research Assistant under Dr. Gokhan Kul on Department of Defense-funded cybersecurity research.

My research focuses on adversarial risk analysis, network intrusion detection systems, and attack chain prediction using the MITRE ATT&CK framework. I've authored three research papers currently under review, collaborating with the U.S. Military Academy on national security applications.

I'm passionate about securing AI systems through adversarial understanding and robust design, with expertise spanning cybersecurity research, machine learning, and software engineering.
